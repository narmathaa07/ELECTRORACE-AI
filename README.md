ELECTRORACE AI


## Smart Energy Intelligence Platform

ElectroTrace AI is a Non-Intrusive Load Monitoring (NILM) system that detects appliance-level electricity usage using a single sensor and AI-based disaggregation.

---

## 🚀 Features

- Appliance-level energy detection (NILM AI)
- Waste detection system
- Energy AI Copilot (chat assistant)
- Prediction of monthly electricity bills
- Energy loss analysis dashboard

---

## 🧠 Technology

- Streamlit
- Python
- AI-based simulation models
- NILM concept (Non-Intrusive Load Monitoring)

---

## 📊 Problem Solved

Helps users reduce electricity waste caused by:
- Air conditioners running in empty rooms
- Idle appliances
- Unnoticed standby power consumption

---

## ⚡ Impact

Reduces electricity bills and promotes energy efficiency in households and small businesses.
